aPhone SE Production Story

2021.12.04

aPhone SE is aPple's first smartphone, and here is the story behind its creation.

Why did you decide to develop aPhone?

Smartphone have become indispensable devices in our daily lives these days. However, I felt that there was something different about those smartphones.
For example, the performance. The performance of even high-end devices has reached a ceiling. The current situation may be the limit. I wondered if I could somehow fix such shortcomings. That's what we thought.
We went through a Trial-and-Error process to see if we could make a smartphone with high performance at a low price. The result was the aPhone SE.

Why did you concentrate on using your own CPU?

As I mentioned earlier, I would like to equip smartphones with performance that can be stretched to infinity. In order to achieve this, I thought that we needed to develop the most suitable CPU for our aPhone.
The development was not an easy task either. ow much high performance could we pack into a small chip? That was the most difficult part.
The CPU used in the aPhone SE is the A10 Bionic CPU. aPple is still in its first stage of development.We are working on development every day, believing that we can make further progress.

What were you particularly conscious of in terms of design?

We wanted a design that was neither too flashy nor too simple to attract attention.So, what kind of design should we make? As a result, we came up with a design like aPhone SE.
It comes in a Variety of colors, Has rounded sides, and a straight back. It isEasy to place, Easy to hold, and Hard to fall off. This design was the best choice for us.
We also paid a lot of attention to the color. We wanted a color that would suit everyone and not get boring. We also wanted a lineup of colors that would be easy to choose and make people want to choose. The five colors are Black, White, Blue, Green, and Purple.

Was there anything you devised for the aOS?

Anyway, we pursued ease of use. First of all, we decided to develop an OS that is agile enough not to be left behind by the ultra-fast operation of aPhone. Then, we decided to develop an OS with excellent security features, so that anything can be done with a smartphone equipped with aOS. These items also absolutely had to be included.
"As far as I can remember, we had to recreate them about fifty times."
The aOS is the result of such repeated Trial and Error.
We are constantly updating this aOS, keeping it at the highest level of security in the world, and making it more useful.

We hope you will continue to support us.
