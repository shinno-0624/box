aWatch 1

Time, Health, Environment, Everything in Between.

aWatch Features

Just by wearing aWatch 1 on your wrist, you can find out the current time, your health condition, air condition, environment condition...
You can know the many things.

Here are some of the main sensors that aWatch 1 is equipped with.

  • Health Care Sensor
  • Breath Sensor
  • Air Watch Sensor
  • Enviro Sensor

The aWatch 1 is also equipped with a variety of other sensors.

It will fit you.

Available in Size L and Size M
Case color: Space Gray or Shiny Gold
There are three types of bands to choose from.

Size L fits wrists from 140~200mm, size M fits wrists from 130~180mm.

The Battery is designed to last 3 days1.

You don't want to charge your watch every day.
Powered by aPple low power Li-ion battery with 3,400mAh Battery capacity and it can be used without charging for up to 73 hours in aPple's experiment23.
It also supports 12V fast charging, and can be charged up to 80% in 80 minutes4.

Just move it closer and beep. Convenient aPay

One of the most notable features of the aWatch 1 is aPay.
The aWatch 1 is equipped with aPay Sensor,
which is a dedicated sensor for aPay and is IC compatible5.

Focus on security features

With aPay, you don't have to worry about payments being made while you are distracted.
To make a payment with aPay, you need to unlock it with your aPhone or enter the 4-digit passcode passcode set in your aWatch 1.
With the same level of security features as other aPple products, there is no need to worry about using the aWatch 1.

Strong screen durability

The screen is made of 80% Glass, 15% Ceramic Shield, 4.75% Plastic and 0.25%Diamond.
Just like the aPhone, the screen is made of Diamond, so it has a very strong display6.
Even if you fall on your bike or in the mountains while running, the screen will not crack7, and can even alert you of your emergency.

aWatch 1 can also communicate on its own

If you purchase the Mobile model of aWatch 1, you can also communicate with aWatch 1 alone8.
Even if you leave your aPhone at home and go for a run or a walk, you can still answer calls and send emails.
Even the unbranded model can communicate as long as it is connected to Wi-Fi.
