Trade-in of aPple products

This page contains information about product trade-ins conducted by aPple.

How the aPple Trade-In System Works

  1. Customers send aPple the products they want to trade-in.
  2. aPple will check the appearance and performance of the product sent by the customer.
  3. We will determine the specific purchase price by deciding which of the following five levels: new and unopened, no scratches, scratched, battery deteriorated, or malfunctioning.
  4. We will inform the customer of the purchase price and send a prepaid card for the purchase amount that can be used to purchase a new aPple product.
  5. The purchased product will be disassembled and recycled into its individual parts. A portion will be donated to medical institutions.

New/Unopened: The product packaging must be unbroken and the device must never have been activated. We usually charge 90% of the list price.
No Scratched: The product must have been opened and no scratches can be found throughout the product. We usually charge 80% of the list price.
Scratched: The product has been opened and has some scratches. Normally, 50% of the list price will be charged.
Battery Deteriorated: For products with a maximum battery capacity of 75% or less. (Maximum battery capacity can be found in Settings > Battery) Normally, 30% of the list price will be charged.
Broken: If the product has one or more broken points (e.g. buttons do not work, unstable operation, etc.), we will buy it at less than 10% of the list price. Depending on the condition of the product, we may not be able to purchase it.

Notice

  • The purchase price will be determined after aPple collects and confirms the product.
  • Peripherals of aPhone are not included in the purchase price.
  • aWatch bands are included in the purchase price. aWatch bands will not be purchased if they are not included in the package when it is sent to aPple.
  • Once the product is sent to aPple, aPple will check the product. Please note that this may result in a different purchase price than what you were expecting.
  • Products that have been disassembled by someone other than aPple cannot be purchased.
  • We may not be able to purchase some products.
