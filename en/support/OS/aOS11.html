aOS 11

Release on December 25. Standard on the new aPhone.

New Features

  • Phone: Supports calls with multiple people at once. Also, the group talk feature for large groups will be available as a beta version.
  • Mail: New design. Dark mode is now supported. You will also be able to have a group talk on the Phone app directly from the Mail app.
  • Maps: More useful maps will be added. It uses the most advanced AI to suggest the best route for you.

Fixed Bugs

  • Fixed an issue where video images may not be displayed correctly when making a video call on the phone.
  • Fixes a bug in memos where the priority is not recorded even if it is set.
